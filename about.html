About Me

Specializing in UI/UX Design, Gameplay Programming, and Project Management

Hello, I'm Alex!

From a young age, I've always had a knack for computers and video games. I was always curious about how games and media were made and why certain aspects resonated with me so strongly. I explored this curiosity by diving into graphic design and video production in high school, which later ignited a passion for game development. I want to continue learning about what makes games so special through game development, and explore the endless possibilities that come with it. I am a more well rounded game designer, and I am highly motivated to keep going and push the limits of the gaming industry. I challenge myself every day, with the goal of creating unique and memorable experiences for players to enjoy all around the world.
