Hello, I'm Alex!

From a young age, I've always had a niche for computers and video games. I was always curious about how games and media were made and why certain aspects resonated with me so much. I explored this curiosity by diving into graphic design and video production in high school, which later ignited a passion for game development. I want to continue learning about what makes games so special through game development, and explore the endless possibilities that come with it. I can confidently say I am a well rounded game designer with more motivation than ever to keep going and push the limits of the gaming industry. I challenge myself every day, with the goal of creating unique and memorable experiences for players to enjoy all around the world.
