Overview

A biblical Hack N' Slash set 3,000 years ago

David's Mighty Men is a 3D Hack N' Slash Historical game set during the reign of King David. I led a team of six as Creative Director through the game's first three months of development up until vertical slice — overseeing UI design, gameplay programming, and overall creative direction.

Vertical Slice Gameplay Showcase

UI/UX Design

Full menu system built with CommonUI

As UI/UX Designer I implemented all UI elements in-game, leveraging Unreal Engine's CommonUI plugin to accomplish my goals. This was the most challenging UI project in my journey so far — and the most rewarding.

  • Main Menu — Start, Continue, Settings, Credits, and Quit with a live animated background
  • Settings Menu — Video, audio, and control settings that persist on apply with reset-to-default
  • Video Settings — Resolution, window mode, VSync, frame rate, and graphics quality
  • Audio Settings — Independent SFX, Music, and Voice Line volume sliders
  • Sensitivity Settings — Mouse and controller sensitivity, fully saveable
  • Level Select — Choose which level to load after starting the game
  • Pause Menu — Continue, Settings, or Quit to Main Menu
  • Full controller compatibility across all menus

Gameplay Programming

Combat system in action — vertical slice build

Full combat system built in two weeks

During the prototyping phase I was given two weeks to program all core player mechanics. Using Blueprints and animation blend trees I delivered a complete foundation for player engagement.

  • Movement — Walk-to-run with synced animation blend trees
  • Shielding & Strafing — Block incoming attacks, move at locked rotation with shield raised
  • Dodging — Directional displacement based on controller input
  • Lock-On — Camera orient to enemy on toggle, released by walking away
  • Quick Attacks — Combo system with 1.5s reset timer, finishes with ground strike
  • Strong Attacks — Slower, momentum-based strikes with aerial finisher
  • Combo Mixing — Switch between quick and strong attacks mid-combo freely
  • Charge Attack — Long buildup into devastating swing
  • Divine Intervention — Cinematic ultimate ability when Divine Meter is full

Reflection

What I learned

When I first started this project, I wanted to blend the worlds of faith and video games together to create something truly unique. I honestly didn't think an idea like this would get past the pitch, let alone make it to vertical slice. To say I learned a lot from this experience would be a huge understatement.

As development progressed I challenged myself every day — learning team leadership, programming, UI design, and more. Most importantly I learned to keep pushing despite the many obstacles and deadlines we faced. With a team of six and only three months in a full production cycle, everyone had to carry multiple roles and get out of their comfort zones. I'm very proud of what we accomplished.
